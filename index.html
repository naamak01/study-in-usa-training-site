Before deciding to study in the USA, you need to ask yourself these questions:

  • Maybe good career prospects
  • Reputation of USA degrees internationally
  • Cultural Diversity
  • Flexible academic system
  • Excellent infrastructures and support Systems
  • Get a list of potential colleges you would be interest in attending
  • Location of colleges ( State, City, Rural ) Very important! This can affect your cost of living, personal life, tuition, and how you adjust to the system and culture here.
  • Be aware that there different types of college ( Private, Public, For-Profit, and many others )
  • Need to make sure your is highly demanded field witha high chance of employment
  • Need to be intentional about your choice. In the USA you can major in any field that you want. There are no restrictions like in other countries
  • Recommendation: Go for the STEM Majors if you can. The possiblity of being able to work for 3 years for bachelors degree and above is high.
  • Need to know if you will have to apply for scholarships
  • How will I finance my education in case I do not get scholarships
  • What ways can I finance my education
  • Is the whole prcess feasible
  • Studying in the USA can get very expensive. Not having a plan can be detrimental

Keep Notes of these. Very Important!!

  • Living in big cities will be more expensive than a smaller or medium size town or city
  • The State Or Region you choose to go to college at can affect you. There are 5 regions ( The Midwest, West, Southwest, SouthEast, and Northeast )
  • Community Colleges are cheaper, and can be a great way to attend college here in the USA. You have a better change of getting scholarships and being able to pay for it. It can be hard to get Visa for Community College
  • Your first Semester or first year will be the toughes if you have to pay for vollege. It is what I call the " Make or Break " period. it is either you able to pay, or have to drop out. Sometimes your I-20 which I will talk about later can get cancelled if you cannot pay. Therefore be compelled to go back home.
